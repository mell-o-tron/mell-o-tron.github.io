The Knights

The Knights who say λ is a group of students, mainly based at the University of Pisa. We organize seminars about theoretical computer science, complex systems, mathematics and frogs. As the name suggests, the founders of the community are hardcore Monty Python fans. If you want to join us, visit the contact page.
